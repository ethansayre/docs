## About Givefront

We're Givefront - a dedicated nonprofit accounting service provider designed to support the financial and operational needs of nonprofit organizations. Our mission is to ensure that your nonprofit operates with financial integrity and compliance, allowing you to focus on your core mission and impact.

### Dedicated Certified Public Accountant (CPA) Assignment
Our clients benefit from the expertise of a dedicated CPA from our seasoned team. This CPA provides tailored and professional accounting support, ensuring that your nonprofit's unique financial needs are met with precision and care.

### Bookkeeping and Financial Compliance
We implement and uphold meticulous bookkeeping practices in accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP). This guarantees that your financial records are not only accurate but also adhere to the highest standards of reliability and integrity.

### Monthly Bookkeeping and Financial Reports
Stay ahead with our comprehensive monthly bookkeeping and financial reports. These regularly updated reports offer clear insights into your financial status, empowering you to make informed, strategic decisions based on the latest data.

### Tax Extension Filing
To assist your nonprofit in meeting critical tax deadlines, we can file the tax extension form 8868 when requested and applicable. This service provides additional time, reducing stress and ensuring compliance with filing requirements.

### Consultation Services for Form 990
Navigating the complexities of Form 990 is simplified with our expert consultation services. We guide you through the preparation and filing process, ensuring your nonprofit meets all IRS requirements efficiently and accurately.

### Nonprofit Audited Financial Statements
We specialize in preparing and filing audited financial statements that meet the stringent standards required by the state of Massachusetts. This ensures full compliance and transparency, providing confidence to your stakeholders and donors.

### Grant Reporting and Advisory Services
Our grant reporting and advisory services offer comprehensive support, helping your nonprofit effectively manage and report on grant funds. We provide strategic advice to maximize grant compliance and success, ensuring that all reporting obligations are met with precision.
